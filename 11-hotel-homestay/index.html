Our Story

A Home Away From Home

Nestled in the serene village of Nagarkot, just 32 km from Kathmandu, Himalayan Haven Homestay offers an authentic experience of Nepali rural life with modern comforts.

Our family has been welcoming guests for over 15 years, sharing our culture, traditions, and the simple joys of mountain living. From our terrace, you can witness spectacular sunrises over the Himalayan range, including views of Mount Everest on clear days.
