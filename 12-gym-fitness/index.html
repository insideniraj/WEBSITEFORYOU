About Us

WHERE FITNESS MEETS EXCELLENCE

Peak Fitness Nepal was founded in 2014 with a mission to bring world-class fitness facilities to Kathmandu. What started as a small gym has grown into Nepal's most trusted fitness center with over 5,000 active members.

Our 15,000 sq. ft. facility features the latest equipment from Life Fitness, Hammer Strength, and Technogym. Whether you're a beginner or a professional athlete, we have everything you need to achieve your fitness goals.
