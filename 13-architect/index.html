About Us

Creating Meaningful Spaces Since 2009

Nepal Design Studio is a full-service architecture and interior design firm based in Kathmandu. Founded by Ar. Sujan Shrestha, we believe that great design has the power to transform lives and communities.

Our multidisciplinary team brings together architects, interior designers, landscape architects, and sustainability consultants to deliver holistic design solutions. We specialize in residential, commercial, and hospitality projects that celebrate local materials and craftsmanship while embracing contemporary design principles.

How We Work

Our Design Process

A systematic approach to bringing your vision to life

01

Discovery

We begin by understanding your vision, requirements, budget, and timeline through detailed consultations.

02

Concept Design

Our team develops initial design concepts, including sketches, mood boards, and preliminary 3D visualizations.

03

Design Development

We refine the chosen concept, developing detailed drawings, material specifications, and cost estimates.

04

Documentation

Complete construction documents are prepared, including technical drawings and specifications for contractors.

05

Construction

We oversee the construction process, ensuring quality control and adherence to the design intent.
