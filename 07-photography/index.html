About Me

Hello, I'm Bikash Chitrakar

A passionate photographer dedicated to capturing life's most precious moments through my lens.

With over 12 years of experience in professional photography, I have had the privilege of documenting hundreds of weddings, corporate events, and personal milestones across Nepal. My approach combines artistic vision with technical expertise to create timeless images that tell your unique story.

Based in Kathmandu, I specialize in wedding photography, portrait sessions, and commercial photography. Every project is an opportunity to create something beautiful and meaningful.
