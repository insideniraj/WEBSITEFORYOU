Our Story

Brewing Happiness Since 2019

The Himalayan Brew was born from a simple dream – to create a space where coffee lovers could escape the chaos of the city and enjoy a perfect cup of coffee.

We source our beans directly from local farmers in Gulmi, Syangja, and Kavre, ensuring fair prices for farmers and the freshest beans for our customers. Every cup is crafted with care by our trained baristas who share our passion for coffee.
