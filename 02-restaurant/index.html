Our Story

A Culinary Journey Through Nepal

Founded in 2009, Himalayan Eatery has been serving authentic Nepali cuisine in the heart of Kathmandu. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, preserving the rich culinary heritage of Nepal.

We source our ingredients from local farmers and use traditional cooking methods to bring you the most authentic flavors. Every dish is prepared with love and served with warmth.
