About Me

Dedicated to Excellence in Cardiac Care

I am Dr. Anil Sharma, a board-certified cardiologist with over two decades of experience in diagnosing and treating heart conditions. After completing my MD from BPKIHS and DM in Cardiology from AIIMS New Delhi, I have dedicated my career to advancing cardiac care in Nepal.

My approach combines cutting-edge medical technology with compassionate patient care. I believe in educating my patients about their conditions and involving them in treatment decisions to achieve the best outcomes.

Qualifications & Training

  • MBBS - Tribhuvan University (1998)
  • MD (Internal Medicine) - BPKIHS (2003)
  • DM (Cardiology) - AIIMS, New Delhi (2007)
  • Fellowship in Interventional Cardiology - Singapore (2010)
NMC Registered Nepal Heart Foundation Cardiological Society of Nepal
What I Offer

Specialized Services

Comprehensive cardiac care using the latest diagnostic and treatment techniques

General Cardiology

Comprehensive evaluation and management of all heart conditions including hypertension, heart failure, and arrhythmias.

Interventional Cardiology

Minimally invasive procedures including coronary angiography, angioplasty, and stent placement.

Echocardiography

Advanced cardiac imaging using 2D, 3D, and stress echocardiography for accurate diagnosis.

Pacemaker Implantation

Permanent pacemaker and ICD implantation for patients with heart rhythm disorders.

Cardiac Rehabilitation

Personalized exercise and lifestyle programs for recovery after heart procedures.

Preventive Cardiology

Risk assessment and prevention strategies for heart disease through lifestyle modification.
