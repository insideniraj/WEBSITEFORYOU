About Us

Your Journey to Beauty Begins Here

Founded in 2014, Serenity Beauty Lounge has been Kathmandu's destination for premium beauty services. Our mission is to help every client discover their inner beauty and leave feeling confident and radiant.

We combine traditional beauty techniques with modern innovations, using only the finest products from renowned international brands. Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to providing personalized services that cater to your unique style and preferences.
