Welcome to Kathmandu Smile Clinic

Established in 2009, Kathmandu Smile Clinic has been providing exceptional dental care to patients across Nepal. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest dental technology to ensure precise diagnoses and effective treatments.

We believe that everyone deserves a beautiful, healthy smile. Our team of highly qualified dentists and friendly staff are committed to making your dental experience comfortable and stress-free.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of dental services to meet all your oral health needs

General Dentistry

Regular check-ups, cleanings, fillings, and preventive care to maintain your oral health.

Dental Implants

Permanent solution for missing teeth with natural-looking dental implants.

Teeth Whitening

Professional whitening treatments to brighten your smile safely and effectively.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Veneers, bonding, and smile makeovers to enhance your appearance.

Orthodontics

Braces and aligners to straighten teeth and correct bite issues.

Root Canal Treatment

Pain-free root canal therapy to save infected teeth.
