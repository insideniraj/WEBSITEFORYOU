About Us

Your Trusted Trekking Partner

Himalayan Trails Adventure was founded in 2010 by a team of passionate mountaineers and trekking enthusiasts. With over 15 years of experience, we have helped thousands of adventurers from around the world experience the magic of the Himalayas.

We are a government-registered trekking agency committed to providing safe, sustainable, and unforgettable trekking experiences. Our team of experienced guides and porters are trained in first aid, high altitude rescue, and environmental conservation.

The Himalayan Trails Difference

Safety First

All guides are trained in first aid and high altitude rescue. We carry emergency equipment on every trek.

Small Groups

Maximum 12 trekkers per group ensures personalized attention and a better experience.

Fair Wages

We pay our guides and porters fair wages and provide proper equipment and insurance.

Eco-Friendly

Leave no trace policy. We carry back all waste and support local conservation efforts.

Best Value

Competitive pricing with no hidden costs. All permits, accommodation, and meals included.

24/7 Support
