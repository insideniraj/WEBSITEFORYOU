Our Story

Growing Organic Since 2015

Himalayan Harvest Organics was founded with a simple mission: to bring the purest, most nutritious organic produce from the fertile hills of Nepal to health-conscious families across the valley.

Our 25-acre farm in Godawari is nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas, where clean air, pure water, and rich soil create the perfect conditions for organic farming. We use traditional farming methods combined with modern sustainable practices to grow over 50 varieties of vegetables, fruits, and herbs.
